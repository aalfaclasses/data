Tution Details

Aalfa Classes is a premier tuition institute based in India, dedicated to providing top-quality education to students from grades 5 to 12. With a strong foundation in both Gujarati and English mediums, we cater to a wide range of academic needs. Our primary focus is on the Science batch for grades 11 and 12, but we also offer comprehensive tuition for Commerce students in these grades. Additionally, we specialize in preparing students for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and GUJCET, ensuring they achieve their highest potential.
